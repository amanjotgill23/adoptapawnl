SPCA St. John's

Location: St. John's

The SPCA St. John's is an adoption-focused agency helping homeless cats and dogs find happy beginnings. They work, encourage, and assist with spaying and neutering across the Avalon Peninsula, helping make animal homelessness a thing of the past.

NL West SPCA

Location: Corner Brook

Established in Corner Brook for over 35 years ago as the Bay of Islands SPCA, their goal is to raise awareness about the abuse and abandonment of animals, to teach and foster good pet parenting practices, encourage community cooperation, recognize and commend the efforts of volunteers, and to promote spay and neuter programs around the province.

Beagle Paws

Location: St. John's

Beagle Paws Rescue began in 2003 to rescue mistreated Beagles. Since then, they have become Canada's largest beagle rescue group, re-homing Beagles right across the country.
